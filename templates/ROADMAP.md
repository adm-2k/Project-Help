## 🎯 Vision

A friendly place to save your recipes and discover your friends'.

## 🔄 Now (in progress this week)

- **Add a recipe** — the form for title, ingredients, and steps (Maya)
- **Browse all recipes** — the list page that shows every saved recipe (Sam)

## ⏭️ Next (coming up)

- **Search by ingredient** — find recipes by typing an ingredient like "tomato" (Priya)
- **Save favorites** — a button to bookmark recipes and a view to see them (Devin)

## 🕰️ Later (deferred on purpose)

These are good ideas we've *chosen* to leave out of v1 to keep it simple:

- User accounts and login
- Photos on recipes
- Ratings
- Comments

## ✅ Done (shipped)

- **Project setup** — repository created, team has access, templates in place — *2026-06-10*
